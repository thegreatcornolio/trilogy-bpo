Case Study

How we transformed collections for HomeChoice

HomeChoice — South Africa's leading home shopping retailer

HomeChoice was relying entirely on outbound voice for collections — nearly 5 million dials a month with a right-party connect rate of just 9.6%. Trilogy identified the opportunity, designed the solution, and led the full implementation — bringing in Infobip for the WhatsApp Business API infrastructure and Codified for the technical build.

The Challenge

  • 5 million voice dials per month reaching only 9.6% right-party connect
  • Customers unreachable through telephony — needing a digital channel
  • Improve decision-maker contact rates at lower cost
  • Reduce collections headcount while increasing collections volume

The Trilogy Solution

A WhatsApp collections solution — scoped, sourced and led by Trilogy — with campaigns enabling agents to handle multiple asynchronous conversations simultaneously, dramatically lifting connect rates from day one.

45%Increase in collections rate by month 3
3MAdditional collections per month from month 3
61%More customers actually contacted
55%Reduction in agents — 108 down to 60

30% increase in revenue & RPC from month one, rising to 45% by month 3. Also achieved: 91% customer satisfaction across digital channels and a 35% reduction in inbound call volumes.

Partners: Infobip (WhatsApp Business API) · Codified (technical implementation). Programme designed, sourced and managed end-to-end by Trilogy.
